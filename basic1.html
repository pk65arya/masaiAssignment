Intoduction to Newtons Law

Law of Inertia(Newtons First Law)

"An object at rest will stay at rest, and an object in motion will stay in motion at a constant velocity unless acted upon by an external force."

Newtons Second Law

"The force acting on an object is equal to the mass of that object multiplied by its acceleration."

Newtons Third Law

"For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction."
